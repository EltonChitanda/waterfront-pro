Our Services

Quality workmanship across Limpopo and Gauteng since 2014.

Building

We provide high quality building services for both residential and commercial properties. From foundations to finishing, our team delivers structures built to last.

Plumbing

Professional plumbing installations, repairs and maintenance for homes and businesses. We handle everything from leaking pipes to full plumbing system installations.

Thatch Roofs

Expert thatch roof installation and maintenance. We use quality materials to create beautiful, durable thatch roofs that stand the test of time.

Harvey Thatch Roofs

Specializing in Harvey thatch roof installations, we bring expertise and precision to every project. Harvey thatch is known for its durability and natural aesthetic.

Thatch Fire Retardant

We apply professional fire retardant treatments to thatch roofs, giving you peace of mind and protecting your property from fire hazards.

Cemecrete Floors

We install smooth, durable cemecrete floors for residential and commercial spaces. Strong, cost effective and low maintenance — ideal for any property.

General Maintenance

From small repairs to full property maintenance, Waterfront Pro has you covered. We keep your property in top condition all year round.
